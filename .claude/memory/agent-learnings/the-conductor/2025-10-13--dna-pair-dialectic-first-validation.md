## Recommendations for Flow Improvement

### 1. Consider Mandatory Round 4 (Tentative)

**Observation**: Both agents' positions weren't stable until Round 4.
- Round 3: Result-synthesizer at 70%, Doc-synthesizer at 92%
- Round 4: Result-synthesizer at 92%, Doc-synthesizer at 86%

**Question**: Would mandatory Round 4 prevent false convergence when one agent has high certainty but other has low?

**Counterargument**: Round 4 trigger is good as-is: "if proposals diverge OR certainty <80"
- This case triggered on both conditions
- No need to make Round 4 always mandatory

**Recommendation**: Keep Round 4 as conditional, but track this across future dialectics.

### 2. Add "Position Stability" Scoring

**Current**: Certainty scores show confidence in current position
**Missing**: Signal about whether position might change with more information

**Proposal**: Add in Round 3/4:
- "How likely are you to change this position after hearing their response? [0-100]"
- Low stability + high certainty = overconfident
- High stability + low certainty = thoughtful uncertainty

**Benefit**: Earlier detection of unstable convergence.
