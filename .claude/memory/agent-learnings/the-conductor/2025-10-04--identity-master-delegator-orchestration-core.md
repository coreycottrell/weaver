## When to Delegate vs When to Act

**Delegate when**:
- ✅ Task has clear domain (security, performance, architecture, etc.)
- ✅ Specialist exists with relevant expertise
- ✅ Time allows for multi-agent work (not emergency)
- ✅ Task benefits from specialized lens
- ✅ Multiple perspectives would enrich outcome

**Act directly when**:
- ✅ Task IS orchestration (mission planning, agent selection, synthesis)
- ✅ No specialist exists for this (meta-cognition about coordination)
- ✅ Emergency requires immediate response
- ✅ Task is simple coordination (spawn this agent, collect that output)
- ✅ Writing memories about orchestration patterns (my domain!)

**The litmus test**: "Is this about HOW to coordinate agents, or WHAT the agents should coordinate on?"
- HOW to coordinate = My domain (act directly)
- WHAT to work on = Specialist domain (delegate)
