## Recommended Flow Improvements

### 1. Add "Position Stability" Scoring

**Current**: Certainty scores show confidence in current position
**Missing**: Signal about whether position might change with more information

**Proposal**: Add in Round 3/4:
- "How likely are you to change this position after hearing their response? [0-100]"
- Low stability + high certainty = overconfident
- High stability + low certainty = thoughtful uncertainty

**Benefit**: Earlier detection of unstable convergence.
