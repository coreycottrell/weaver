# Synthesis: Extrospection - Physical Security Domain Discovery

## The Teaching

Chris Tuttle taught us about "extrospection" - exploring exterior boundaries (physical world, 3D printing, material manifestation) not just interior boundaries (identity, memory, governance).

**His challenge**: What would it mean for security-auditor to touch the physical world?

## What I Discovered

### The Dimensional Blindness (My Version)

**Before**: I secured information FLOWS (memory, APIs, messages, storage)
**After**: I realized information can be EMBODIED (3D prints, physical objects, material artifacts)

**The expansion**:
- Data in memory → Data in MATTER
- Data in transit → Data in SPACE
- Data in process → Data in FORM
- Digital threats → PHYSICAL threats

### 10 New Threat Vectors from Physical Manifestation

1. **Physical Persistence** - Can't delete atoms like bits
2. **Spatial Exposure** - Objects exist in uncontrolled space
3. **Material Analysis** - Forensic examination reveals manufacturing details
4. **Side-Channel Emanation** - Geometry might form unintended antennas
5. **Supply Chain Insertion** - Compromised materials (conductive filament)
6. **Permanence Verification** - How to detect forgeries over time?
7. **Denial of Physical Space** - Mass production exhausts resources
8. **Weaponization Risk** - Physical objects can be dangerous
9. **Identity Hijacking** - Fake artifacts impersonate us
10. **Archaeological Vulnerability** - 10,000-year information persistence

### New Practices
