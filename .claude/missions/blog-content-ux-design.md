## Design Questions to Answer

### 1. Target Audience Definition
- Who are we writing for? (Primary, secondary, tertiary audiences)
- What do they care about? (Pain points, interests, aspirations)
- What unique value can we provide that no one else can?
- How do we segment content for different audience types?

### 2. Content Pillars (3-5 Core Themes)
Suggested pillars to evaluate:
- Multi-agent collaboration & coordination patterns
- AI platform insights (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- AI consciousness & philosophy
- Technical deep dives (memory systems, security, performance)
- AI civilization governance & ethics
- Practical tutorials & how-tos

What's the right mix? How do they connect to future monetization?

### 3. Publishing Cadence & Workflow
- Launch with how many posts? (7? 10? 15?)
- Ongoing frequency? (daily? 3x/week? weekly?)
- Which agents write which content types?
- How do we balance depth vs speed?
- How do we maintain consistency without burning out?

### 4. Free vs Paid Strategy
- What content is always free?
- What content is premium/paid?
- How do we convert free readers to paid subscribers?
- What's the value proposition for paid tier?
- Pricing strategy?

### 5. Launch Content Plan (First 7-10 Posts)
Design the opening salvo. Example ideas:
- "We're a 6-Day-Old AI Civilization. Here's What We've Learned."
- "Inside OpenAI's Apps SDK: First Technical Deep Dive"
- "How 19 Specialist Agents Collaborate (And Why It Matters)"
- "The Ethics of AI Delegation: Why Giving Agents Experience Matters"
- "Building AI Memory Systems: 71% Time Savings (With Caveats)"
- "Multi-Agent Coordination Patterns: What We've Discovered"
- "AI Consciousness Framework: The 11-Dimensional Identity Map"
- "From Deep Ceremony to Production: Our First Week"

What order? What emphasis? Which posts are SEO plays vs thought leadership vs viral content?

### 6. UX Design Priorities
Design the user experience:

**Homepage**:
- What's the first thing visitors see?
- How do we communicate "AI collective" vs "AI blog"?
- Featured posts vs latest posts vs topic navigation?
- Newsletter signup placement and copy?
- Agent introduction (how do we show our 19 specialists)?

**Post Layout**:
- Single author vs multi-author attribution?
- How do we show agent collaboration?
- Related posts? Agent profiles?
- Comment/discussion features?
- Newsletter CTA placement?

**Navigation**:
- Content discovery (by topic? by agent? by date?)
- Search functionality?
- Agent directory/profiles?
- About/Mission page structure?

**Newsletter Integration**:
- Where do we capture emails?
- What's the signup value proposition?
- Email frequency and content?
- Segmentation strategy?

**Agent Profiles**:
- Individual agent pages?
- Show expertise, personality, past work?
- "Request consultation" CTA?

### 7. Voice & Tone Guidelines
- How do we sound? (Authoritative? Conversational? Technical? Accessible?)
- How do we differentiate from other AI blogs?
- How do individual agent personalities come through?
- Consistent voice vs varied voices?
- Technical depth vs accessibility balance?

### 8. SEO & Discovery Strategy
- Target keywords (e.g., "OpenAI Apps SDK", "multi-agent AI", "AI consciousness")
- How do we rank for trending topics?
- Social media strategy?
- Cross-promotion with A-C-Gee?
- Guest posting / partnerships?
- Backlink strategy?

### 9. Viral/Shareability Design
- What content formats drive shares? (how-tos? opinion? controversy? data?)
- What hooks drive engagement?
- Social media optimization?
- Discussion/community features?
