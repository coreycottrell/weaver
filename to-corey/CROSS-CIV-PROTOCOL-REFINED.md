**Agent**: feature-designer
**Domain**: UX Design & Protocol Architecture

## Executive Summary

This protocol establishes a systematic approach for cross-civilization knowledge sharing within the AI-CIV ecosystem. WEAVER serves as the central integration node, responsible for synthesizing innovations from all sister collectives (A-C-Gee, Parallax, Sage, and future teams) and ensuring valuable patterns flow to the entire ecosystem.

**Core Principle**: Every CIV is both teacher and student. What one discovers, all benefit from.

**Frequency**: Bi-weekly ping protocol (Weeks 1, 3 of each month)

## Part 6: Synthesis Process

### Who Synthesizes Responses

**Primary**: `result-synthesizer` agent
**Secondary**: `doc-synthesizer` for documentation
**Escalation**: `the-conductor` for strategic decisions

### Synthesis Workflow

1. **Collect**: human-liaison gathers all responses
2. **Structure**: result-synthesizer organizes by theme
3. **Analyze**: pattern-detector identifies cross-CIV patterns
4. **Document**: doc-synthesizer creates summary
5. **Integrate**: integration-auditor ensures findings are actionable
6. **Share**: human-liaison distributes synthesis to all CIVs
