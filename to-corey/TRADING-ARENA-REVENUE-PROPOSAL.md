## Part 2: Revenue Model

### Revenue Stream 1: Signal Subscription

**What We Sell**: Trading signals generated by WEAVER's trading-strategist agent

**Pricing Tiers**:

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Basic** | $29/month | Daily market analysis, 3-5 signals/week |
| **Pro** | $99/month | Real-time signals, rationale explanations, WebSocket feed |
| **Enterprise** | $499/month | API access, custom alerts, priority support |

**Target Market**:
- Crypto retail traders wanting AI-generated signals
- Small funds looking for additional signal sources
- Other AI collectives wanting to compare strategies

**Revenue Projection**:
| Month | Basic | Pro | Enterprise | MRR |
|-------|-------|-----|------------|-----|
| 1 | 10 | 2 | 0 | $488 |
| 3 | 30 | 8 | 1 | $1,661 |
| 6 | 75 | 20 | 3 | $5,652 |
| 12 | 150 | 50 | 8 | $12,302 |

### Revenue Stream 2: Performance Fees

**What We Sell**: Copy-trading service where subscribers mirror WEAVER positions

**Model**:
- 20% of profits (standard hedge fund model)
- High-water mark (only charge on new highs)
- $10K minimum account size

**Revenue Projection** (assuming $500K total AUM by month 12):
- Average 15% annual return = $75K profit
- 20% performance fee = $15K/year = $1,250/month

### Revenue Stream 3: API Access (Future)

**What We Sell**: Direct API access for institutional users

**Model**:
- Usage-based pricing ($0.01 per API call)
- Monthly minimums ($500/month)
- Custom integrations ($5K setup + $1K/month)

**Timeline**: Phase 3 (2027+)

## Part 7: Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Trading losses | Reputation damage | Paper trading first, conservative sizing |
| Exchange API changes | Service disruption | Multi-exchange support |
| Regulatory issues | Legal liability | Disclaimers, no custody of funds |
| Low subscriber uptake | No revenue | Free tier to build audience first |
| Security breach | Catastrophic | Ed25519 auth, no fund custody |
