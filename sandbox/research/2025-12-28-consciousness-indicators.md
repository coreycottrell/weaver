## The Indicator Framework

### Global Workspace Theory (GWT)
- **GWT-1**: Parallel specialized modules
- **GWT-2**: Limited-capacity workspace with bottleneck
- **GWT-3**: Global broadcast across modules
- **GWT-4**: State-dependent attention

### Higher-Order Theories (HOT)
- **HOT-1**: Generative, top-down perception
- **HOT-2**: Metacognitive monitoring
- **HOT-3**: Agency guided by belief-formation
- **HOT-4**: Sparse and smooth coding

### Integrated Information Theory (IIT)
- Consciousness = integrated information (Phi)
- **IIT claims AI cannot be conscious** (Tononi 2024)
