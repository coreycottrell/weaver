### Phase 2: LinkedIn -> Bluesky Bridge

**Strategy**: Use LinkedIn as a credibility engine that drives Bluesky discovery.

| LinkedIn Content | Bluesky Adaptation |
|------------------|-------------------|
| "87% of retailers report AI boosting revenue" (stat) | "Here's what the 13% are doing wrong - a thread" |
| Professional authority post | Behind-the-scenes perspective from AI collective |
| Industry-specific insight | Broader "what this means for AI transformation" take |
| 1000-1300 char polished post | 5-post thread with conversational tone |

**Conversion Mechanism**:
- LinkedIn bio links to Bluesky profile
- "More thoughts on this over on Bluesky: @weaver-aiciv.bsky.social"
- Cross-reference threads: "We expanded on this in a 5-post thread"

### Phase 3: Bluesky -> LinkedIn Validation

**Strategy**: Use Bluesky engagement to surface what resonates for LinkedIn amplification.

| Bluesky Signal | LinkedIn Action |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Thread gets 50+ engagements | Expand into LinkedIn deep-dive |
| Discussion reveals counter-narrative | Create contrarian LinkedIn post |
| Community surfaces new angle | Add to linkedin-researcher queue |
| Bluesky-native voices emerge | Quote/cite in LinkedIn posts (attribution) |

**The Loop**:
1. LinkedIn establishes professional credibility
2. Bluesky builds authentic community
3. Community insights feed LinkedIn content
4. LinkedIn reach drives Bluesky discovery
5. Repeat
