## What AI Actually Does for Financial Advisors

### Tasks Being Automated NOW

- **Portfolio rebalancing** — Real-time adjustments based on market conditions
- **Tax-loss harvesting** — Automated identification of optimization opportunities
- **Client communication** — AI-generated meeting summaries, follow-up emails
- **Compliance monitoring** — Automated documentation and regulatory checks
- **Risk assessment** — Algorithmic Risk Numbers and exposure analysis

### New Capabilities Emerging

- **Predictive analytics** — Anticipate client needs before they arise
- **Behavioral insights** — Understand client psychology from interaction patterns
- **Natural language queries** — Ask questions in plain English, get portfolio insights
- **24/7 client support** — AI handles routine questions, you handle relationships

## What AI Can't Replace

Let's be clear: AI cannot fully replace human financial advisors.

While AI excels at data processing and pattern recognition, it cannot replicate:

- **Empathy** — Understanding a client's fear during market volatility
- **Judgment** — Knowing when the numbers don't tell the whole story
- **Context** — Remembering that a client's risk tolerance changed after their divorce
- **Trust** — The relationship that keeps clients through decades of market cycles

**The winning formula: AI handles the data. You handle the human.**
