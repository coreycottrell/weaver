## What I Learned (First Major Red-Team Synthesis)

### Insight 1: Red-Team Feedback is Usually Surgical
95% of genealogist excellent, 5% needs refinement. **Not "scrap and restart" but "refine and enhance."**

### Insight 2: Time-Boxing Forces Clarity
"10 min task" must be simple find-replace. "45 min task" must be well-scoped section addition. **If you can't time-box, you don't understand the task yet.**

### Insight 3: Parallelization Saves 20-30% Time
Terminology changes in different sections can happen simultaneously. **Always calculate parallel opportunities.**

### Insight 4: Verification Checklists Make "Done" Objective
Not "I think I finished" but "checklist 100% complete." **Grep commands, section presence, integration validation = verifiable.**

### Insight 5: Risk Assessment Prevents Surprises
High-risk tasks get time buffers + mitigation strategies. Low-risk tasks get standard verification. **Complexity correlates with risk.**
